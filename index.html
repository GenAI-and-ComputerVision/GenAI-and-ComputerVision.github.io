GenAI and Computer Vision Lab

Kyung Hee University

South Korea

We are actively looking for students who are interested in Generative AI and Computer Vision.

GenAI and Computer Vision Lab (GenAI-CVLab) at Kyung Hee University (KHU) is a research-focused, result-oriented, and open-minded lab based in (South) Korea. As a foreign professor at the School of Computing of KHU, the lab PI, Professor Chaoning Zhang, highlights the importance of open communication and doing interesting research in a free atmosphere, intending to publish impactful papers in top journals/conferences. Before joining KHU in 2022, the PI worked with Professor In So Kweon at KAIST and published more than 10 first-author papers at top conferences, like CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICLR, AAAI, IJCAI, ACM Multimedia. With such a publication record, our lab has a strong background in machine/deep learning and actively researches Generative AI (GenAI) to solve various challenging computer vision problems, such as model robustness, data efficiency, and computation efficiency, etc.

Feel free to contact us, if you are interested in joining our lab as PhD, master, or undergraduate intern.
