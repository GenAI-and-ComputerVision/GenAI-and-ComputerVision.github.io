About The Raising

Professor Chaoning Zhang currently holds the position of an assistant professor at Kyung Hee University.

Before assuming this role, Chaoning Zhang gained valuable experience working under the guidance of Professor In So Kweon at KAIST. His research focus spans various areas, including (but not limited to): (1) mode robustness, with an emphasis on adversarial attack/defense, (2) data efficiency, with a particular interest in self-supervised learning, and (3) generative AI, specifically AIGC. In recognition of their contributions, Professor Chaoning Zhang was awarded the Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship (Korea) in 2020. Over the past few years, Professor Chaoning Zhang has authored approximately 30 first or co-first author papers presented at prestigious AI conferences, including CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, ICLR, AAAI, IJCAI, ACM MM, BMVC, WACV, and others.
